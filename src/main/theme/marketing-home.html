International Health Terminology Standards Development Organisation

The International Health Terminology Standards Development Organisation is an international not-for-profit organization based in Denmark. IHTSDO owns and administers the rights to SNOMED CT and related terminology standards.

SNOMED Clinical Terms (SNOMED CT) is the most comprehensive, multilingual clinical healthcare terminology in the world.

    SNOMED CT is

  • a clinical healthcare terminology
  • a resource with comprehensive, scientifically-validated content
  • essential for electronic health records
  • a terminology that can cross-map to other international standards
  • already used in more than fifty countries
