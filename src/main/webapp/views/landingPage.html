About

The International Health Terminology Standards Development Organisation (IHTSDO) is a not-for-profit association that develops and promotes the use of SNOMED CT to support safe and effective health information exchange. For more information on the IHTSDO and SNOMED CT, please go to www.ihtsdo.org.
